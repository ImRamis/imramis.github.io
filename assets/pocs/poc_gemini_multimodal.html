About This Collection

This portfolio represents five years of wildlife photography across six continents. Each image was captured in the animal's natural habitat with minimal human intervention, following ethical wildlife photography guidelines established by the International League of Conservation Photographers.

Camera equipment used includes Nikon Z9, Canon R5, Sony A1, and Fujifilm GFX100, with various telephoto lenses ranging from 100-600mm. All images are minimally processed to preserve the authentic character of each encounter.

Conservation Note: 10% of all print sales from this collection are donated to the World Wildlife Fund and local conservation efforts in each region photographed.
