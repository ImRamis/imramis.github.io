🛒 Buying Guide: What to Look For

When shopping for a budget smartphone in 2026, consider these key factors:

  • Display Quality: Look for AMOLED displays with at least 90Hz refresh rate
  • Processor: Mid-range chips like Snapdragon 7 Gen 3 or Tensor G3 offer excellent performance
  • Camera: A 50MP main sensor with OIS provides great photos
  • Battery: 5,000mAh or more ensures all-day battery life
  • Software Updates: Prioritize phones with 4+ years of updates
